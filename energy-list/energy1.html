Understanding your energy bill

Information you will need to switch your supplier.

Customer Reference Number

This is your personal reference number which you’ll need when you contact your supplier.

What you owe

This will either be an estimated price or actual (you can find this out on your bill). If you think the amount is wrong (for example it has gone up and you’re not sure why), you can check if your bill is correct. To be able to do this, you’ll need the following things, which you can find on your bill:

  • your previous and current meter readings
  • your meter reference number

Personal projection

This is an estimate of how much you’ll spend on energy in the next 12 months with your current supplier. You can use this to compare other tariffs from your supplier and others to make sure you’re on the best deal. There are also other ways to save money on your bills.
